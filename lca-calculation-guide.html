Scope 2: Indirect Energy Emissions

Emissions from purchased electricity you consume.

Electricity (kWh)
Total annual electricity consumption. Sum all monthly utility bills for the year. Include lighting, HVAC, elevators, kitchen equipment, and guest rooms.
Example: 500,000 kWh/year for a 100-room hotel with restaurant and pool

Scope 3: Other Indirect Emissions

Emissions from waste disposal and water treatment in your supply chain.

Waste Sent to Landfill (kg)
Annual weight of solid waste sent to landfills. Check invoices from waste haulers or weigh a typical week and multiply by 52.
Example: 25,000 kg/year (approximately 500 kg/week for a mid-size hotel)
Recycling (kg)
Annual weight of materials recycled (paper, cardboard, glass, plastic, metal). Recycling has lower emissions than landfill.
Example: 8,000 kg/year if recycling 30% of waste stream
Composting (kg)
Annual weight of organic waste composted (food scraps, yard waste). Composting has the lowest emissions of waste options.
Example: 5,000 kg/year for hotels with food waste diversion programs
Water Consumption (m¬≥)
Total annual water use including guest rooms, laundry, kitchen, landscaping, and pools. Check water utility bills.
Example: 15,000 m¬≥/year (approximately 40 m¬≥/day for a 100-room hotel)
Wastewater (m¬≥)
Volume of wastewater sent to treatment facilities. Typically 80-90% of water consumption.
Example: 13,000 m¬≥/year (approximately 87% of water consumption)
